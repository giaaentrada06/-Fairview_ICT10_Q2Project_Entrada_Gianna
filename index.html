Operation Brotherhood Montessori

Operation Brotherhood Montessori (OBMC) is a private educational institution committed to developing students academically, morally, and socially. The school provides quality education through a supportive learning environment that encourages discipline, responsibility, and respect.

OBMC values holistic formation by promoting academic excellence, leadership, and participation in various school clubs and activities. The school aims to prepare students to become responsible and productive members of society.
